Eligibility and Intake

  • Candidates should have completed the requirements of a B.Tech/B.E degree in CSE/IT or an M.Sc degree in Mathematics.

  • Candidates must have a valid GATE score in CS or MA papers.

  • Intake is 12 regular students and 1 student under defence quota.
